Happy 16th Monthsary, Mi Estrella

 

In you, my love, I have found,

A heart that beats with a gentle sound,

Your touch is like the softest breeze,

That brings me to my knees.

 

Your smile, like the rising sun,

Brings warmth to me, and everyone,

Your eyes, like the stars above,

Shine with the light of your love.

 

Your voice, like a lullaby,

Soothes me, as it gently sighs,

Your laugh, like a melody,

Fills my heart with sweet harmony.

 

In every moment that we share,

I find a love that's beyond compare,

And in your arms, I am home,

Never again to be alone.

 

For you are my heart, my soul, my all,

The one I'll cherish, until the final call,

And every day, my love will grow,

In ways that only you will know.
